# Week 2: Blockchain Engine, Smart Contracts and Governance

## Monday: Blockchain and Web3 Fundamentals Deep Dive

Objectives:
- Gain deeper technical understanding beyond buzzwords
- Understand consensus mechanisms: PoW vs PoS simplified
- Learn about gas, blocks, confirmations
- Understand wallet types: custodial vs non-custodial
- Learn about hot vs cold wallets and security risks
- Understand what testnets are and practice preview
- Explore Etherscan and explain components

## Deliverable: Etherscan Transaction Analysis

Explain a transaction on Etherscan in your GitHub repo.

Steps:
1. Go to etherscan.io
2. Find an interesting transaction
3. Document the transaction details:
   - Transaction hash
   - From address
   - To address
   - Amount transferred
   - Gas used
   - Block number
   - Status
4. Explain what happened in this transaction
5. Add your analysis to your "My Web3 Journey" repo

## Wednesday: Smart Contracts 101: The Trustless Engine

Objectives:
- Understand what smart contracts are
- Learn how smart contracts work: functions, triggers
- Understand creative impact: NFT royalties, DAOs, membership passes
- Learn what happens when a contract executes: transaction flow
- Understand the trilemma: Scalability vs Security vs Decentralization

## Deliverable: Smart Contract Case Study

Explain a real smart contract case study in your README.md.

Choose one:
- An NFT collection contract
- A DAO governance contract
- A DeFi protocol contract
- A membership pass contract

Document:
- What the contract does
- Key functions
- How it impacts creatives
- Why it matters

Add your case study to your "My Web3 Journey" repo.

## Friday: DAOs and Discord Management Guest Session

Objectives:
- Learn what a DAO is: ownership vs participation
- Understand governance tokens and proposal systems
- Learn contributor reward models: task-based, role-based
- Understand Discord as coordination HQ: tasks, moderation, channel logic
- Learn role structures: mods, contributors, storytellers, proposal leads
- Study case studies: Friends With Benefits, Forefront, Krause House

## Deliverable: DAO Simulation Participation

Participate in the DAO simulation activity during the session.

Document your participation:
- What role you took
- What proposal you made or voted on
- What you learned about governance
- Add your reflection to your "My Web3 Journey" repo

## How to Submit Your Assignment

Follow these steps:

1. Update your fork of this repository
2. Create a new branch for your submission:
   git checkout -b week-2-assignment

3. Add your work inside the submissions folder:
   submissions/
       └── week-2/
           └── your-name/
               ├── etherscan-analysis.md
               ├── smart-contract-case-study.md
               └── dao-simulation-reflection.md

4. Include links to your "My Web3 Journey" repo where relevant

5. Commit and push your changes:
   git add .
   git commit -m "chore: submit week 2 assignment"
   git push origin week-2-assignment

6. Create a Pull Request to the main repository. Your PR includes:
   - A brief summary of what you learned
   - Links to your deliverables in your personal repo

7. Post at least one reflection on social media about your Week 2 learnings
